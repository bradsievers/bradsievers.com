About

Meet Dr. Sievers

I’m a provisionally licensed psychologist who provides individual therapy to adults across Iowa. I work with clients who are struggling with a range of mental health challenges, and I have advanced training and experience in treating post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD), and anxiety disorders. I believe the foundation of effective therapy is a collaborative and trusting therapeutic relationship where clients’ goals are prioritized and their values and identities are respected and affirmed.

I practice at Willow Grove Therapy Group in Johnston, Iowa, and offer both in-person and telehealth sessions.
